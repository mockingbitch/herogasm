# AI Instructions

When generating economy systems:

- Every resource must have sources and sinks.
- Prevent inflation.
- Make old content remain useful.
- Encourage crafting and upgrading.
- Avoid pay-to-win.
- Design for long-term progression.
- Balance active and idle play.
- Keep economy data-driven.
