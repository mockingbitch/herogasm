# Pixel Art Direction

## Vision

Create a vibrant living medieval fantasy world.

The art style should feel warm, alive and handcrafted.

The player should immediately recognize:

- Busy town
- Autonomous heroes
- Living ecosystem
- Cozy fantasy
- Constant movement

The world must always appear active.
